Gestures

General

swipe down: undo

swipe up: redo

swipe right or left: change mode

implode: reset

explode: exit AR

Place Volume

double tap: place volume on cursor or hides it

pan: rotate volume

pinch: scale volume

twist: roll volume

hold: translate volume in space

Inspect Volume

pan: rotate planes

twist: rotate all 3 planes

hold: translate plane parallel to its direction

hold intersection of planes: translate all 3 planes together

double tap: hide or reveal a plane

pinch: scale volume

Edit Volume

pan: rotate volume

twist: change image contrast

hold: edit mask

double tap: change brush mode

pinch: scale volume

Segment Volume

pan: rotate volume

twist: roll volume

pinch: scale volume

hold: move z plane

single tap: place point to the z-plane and begin segmentation

double tap: change mode between include or exclude point in segmentation mask

implode: remove all points and clear segmentation mask
